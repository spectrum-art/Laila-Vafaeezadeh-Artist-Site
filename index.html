About the Author

Laila Vafaeezadeh was born and raised in the greater Seattle area, surrounded by the beauty of evergreen forests. From a young age, she showed a passion for writing and storytelling. She has worked with animals for over a decade and loves collecting unique and beautiful objects. Writing remains her first love and a driving force in her life.
